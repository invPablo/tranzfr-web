Tranzfr — Account Deletion

Last updated: June 25, 2026

To request deletion of your Tranzfr account and associated data, send an email to support@tranzfr.com from the email address registered with your account, with the subject "Delete my Tranzfr account".

Request account deletion

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (name, email, login credentials)
  • Your membership in any trip groups
  • Expenses, payments, and budgets you created, where deleting them does not affect other members' shared group history

We process deletion requests within 30 days of receiving them.

What may be retained

Expense and payment records that are shared with other members of a group (e.g. an expense you logged that other members' balances depend on) may be retained in anonymized form so the group's shared history stays accurate for the other members. No data identifying you personally is kept beyond what's needed to comply with legal or accounting obligations.

Questions

Contact support@tranzfr.com for anything related to your data or this request.
